Poverty Correlations

Poverty vs Lack of Healthcare

There is a weak coorelation between poverty rates and lack of healthcare. A person is slightly more likely to be uninsured if they are in poverty. There are cases where a state has a higher poverty rate than another state but their percentage of people without healthcare is lower (e.g., Mississippi vs. Texas).
