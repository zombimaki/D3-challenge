Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

By @zombimaki

Poverty Correlations

Poverty vs Lack of Healthcare

There is a slght coorelation between poverty rates and the percentage of people without healthcare. States with a higher poverty rate are more likely to have a higher rate of uninsured people as well.

Poverty vs Smoking

There is a strong coorelation between poverty rates and the percentage of people who are smokers. A state's percentge of smokers becomes greater as the poverty rate of the state's population increases.

Poverty vs Obesity

There is a strong coorelation between poverty rates and the percentage of people who are obese. A state's obesity rate becomes higher as the poverty rate of the state's population increases.

Age Correlations

Age vs Lack of Healthcare

There does not appear to be a strong coorelation between a state's median age and the percentage of the state's population without healthcare.

Age vs Smoking

There does appear to be a strong coorelation between a state's median age and the percentage of of people who smoke. A state's percentge of smokers becomes higher as the median age of the population increases.

Age vs Obesity

There does not appear to be a strong coorelation between a state's median age and the state's obesity rate.

Household Income Correlations

Household Income vs Lack of Healthcare

There is a strong coorelation between household income and the percentage of people without healthcare. States with a lower median household income are more likely to have a higher rate of uninsured people.

Household Income vs Smoking

There does appear to be a strong coorelation between a state's median household income and the percentage of of people who smoke. A state's percentge of smokers becomes lower as the median household income of the state's population increases.

Household Income vs Obesity

There is a strong coorelation between a state's median household income and the percentage of of people who are obese. A state's obesity rate becomes lower as the median household income of the state's population increases.
